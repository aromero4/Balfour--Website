Balfour-Hesburgh Summer Institute

Details

The BHSP Summer Institute at Notre Dame is designed to jump-start our Scholars’ transition to college life and their acculturation to the demanding academic environment of a top-20 university.

During this four-week residential experience, students complete a combination of academic coursework and personal development courses. They will be introduced to the vast resources Notre Dame offers, as well as the level of work that will be required in the fall.

What

Students will attend five classes during the week and various team building activities on the weekend, all aimed at both academic, social, and emotional growth

Why

The Summer Program will help prepare students for the college life, as well as provide a caring community from day one

How

Balfour will coordinate travel and cover all costs (including summer tuition, housing and meals) to attend the Summer Institute.

When

Students will spend four weeks in dorms here at Notre Dame. (June 28th - July 26th)
